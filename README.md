#  Transport Operations Dashboard – Power BI Project

## **Description**
A Power BI Transport Operations Dashboard analyzing fleet performance using on-time delivery %, fuel efficiency trends, vehicle maintenance cost, delivery status, and AI visuals (Q&A, Key Influencers, Decomposition Tree) to optimize routes and improve fleet usage.

---

##  **Key Features**

### ** On-Time Delivery Performance**
- Bar chart showing delivery efficiency by destination  
- Highlights delays, early deliveries, and route performance

### ** Fuel Efficiency Trend**
- Line chart visualizing fuel usage over time  
- Helps identify vehicles with rising fuel consumption

### ** Cost Insights**
- **Average Cost per Km** (DAX measure)  
- Pie chart for **vehicle-type maintenance cost comparison**  
- Cost drivers broken down using advanced visuals

### ** AI Visuals (Power BI Artificial Intelligence Features)**
- **Q&A Visual:**  
  - Natural language query: *“Average cost per km by vehicle type?”*
- **Key Influencers Visual:**  
  - Identifies factors affecting delivery status (distance, vehicle type, driver)
- **Decomposition Tree:**  
  - Breaks down cost per km by maintenance cost, distance, and vehicle type

---

## **Purpose**
This dashboard helps transportation and logistics teams:
- Improve route planning  
- Monitor vehicle performance  
- Reduce fuel & maintenance costs  
- Understand delivery success patterns  
- Make data-driven operational decisions  

---

## **Tools Used**
- **Power BI Desktop**  
- **DAX Measures**  
- **Data Modeling**  
  - Trip Data  
  - Vehicle Master  

---

## **Conclusion**

The Transport Operations Dashboard provides clear insights into delivery efficiency, fuel usage, and maintenance costs, helping teams quickly identify performance issues and cost drivers. With advanced AI visuals like Key Influencers and the Decomposition Tree, the dashboard enables deeper analysis and supports smarter route planning and fleet optimization. Overall, this project delivers a strong data-driven foundation for improving transport operations and reducing operational costs.
